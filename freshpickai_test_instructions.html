FreshPick AI – Bot Testing Instructions

Overview

Welcome to the testing phase of FreshPick AI! We're excited to have you onboard as we continue to refine and improve our WhatsApp-based produce selection bot. Please note that we are currently in development mode, and as such, WhatsApp imposes certain restrictions that prevent unverified numbers from sending and receiving messages. Below are the instructions on how you can gain access and participate in the testing.

Requesting Access

To get started with testing FreshPick AI, please follow these steps:

  1. Email Your Request: Send an email to summyfeb12@gmail.com with the subject line "FreshPick AI Testing Request". In the email, please include your WhatsApp phone number.

  2. Verification Process: Once we receive your request, you will be sent a One-Time Password (OTP) via WhatsApp. This OTP is necessary to verify your number and allow you to interact with the FreshPick AI bot.

  3. Complete the Verification: Enter the OTP you receive into the verification prompt provided by FreshPick AI. This will complete your registration and enable you to start testing the bot.

Testing Instructions

Once you’re verified, you can begin testing FreshPick AI by following these steps:

  1. Open WhatsApp: Start a new chat with the FreshPick AI bot.
  2. Send a Photo: Take a picture of any produce item (e.g., avocado, banana, apple) and send it to the bot.
  3. Receive Feedback: The bot will analyze your image and provide instant feedback on the quality and ripeness of the produce.
  4. Try Different Items: Test with various fruits and vegetables to see how FreshPick AI responds. Your feedback is invaluable!

Important Note

We are actively working on getting our business account approved by WhatsApp, which will soon allow anyone to test the bot without the need for verification. We appreciate your patience during this development phase.

Feedback and Support

If you encounter any issues or have suggestions to improve FreshPick AI, please don’t hesitate to contact us at summyfeb12@gmail.com. Your input is crucial in helping us perfect the bot.

Thank you for being a part of this journey!
